ABOUT

Kaditsa is a platform focused on presenting designers from across Asia within a clear and professional framework.

The platform supports independent designers and small design studios by creating opportunities to present their work, connect with audiences, and build international visibility.

DESIGNER WEEK

Designer Week is a two-day exhibition where selected designers present their work to audiences, buyers, and industry visitors.

  • Up to 25 designers, each presenting selected pieces
  • VIP Preview Night + Public Exhibition Day
  • Private Buyer Preview — invitation only
  • Pre-order based sales model
  • Engagement with media, design institutions, and cultural partners
STRUCTURE

Kaditsa operates with defined roles and a clear production structure designed for stability and responsible growth.

The project works with venue partners, sponsors, and institutional collaborators during the early development phase.

PROJECT

Kaditsa is an independent initiative based in Lisbon, developed with the aim of building a credible platform for Asian designers over time.
