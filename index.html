ABOUT

Kaditsa is an independent cultural platform focused on presenting Asian creative work within a structured, professional framework.

The platform is built to support long-term continuity through financial clarity, operational discipline, and defined execution roles.

DESIGNER WEEK

Designer Week is a two-day selected showcase structured for quality control and measurable performance.

  • Up to 25 designers, each presenting 10 featured designs
  • VIP Preview Night + Public Exhibition Day
  • Private B2B Buyer Preview — By invitation-only.
  • Pre-order driven sales model
  • Engagement strategy includes outreach to ambassadors, media representatives, and institutional partners

STRUCTURE

Kaditsa is built around defined execution roles and a controlled financial framework. Production is designed to be sponsorship-supported, with administrative legal hosting during the establishment phase.

Objective: stability before scale.

PROJECT LEAD

Kaditsa is initiated and structured by an independent project lead currently based in Lisbon. The focus remains on building a credible platform rather than personal visibility.
