ABOUT

Kaditsa is an Asian design platform presenting independent designers and small design studios from across the region through curated exhibitions, showcases, and industry-facing formats.

The platform creates opportunities for designers working across fashion, objects, and contemporary design to present their work, connect with new audiences, and build international visibility.

DESIGN WEEK BY KADITSA

Design Week by Kaditsa is a two-day exhibition where selected designers present their work to audiences, buyers, and invited industry guests.

  • Up to 20 designers presenting selected pieces
  • VIP preview night and public exhibition day
  • Private buyer preview by invitation
  • Pre-order based sales model
  • Engagement with media, design institutions, and cultural partners

The first edition will take place in Lisbon, Portugal, with future formats and editions to be developed over time.

STRUCTURE

Kaditsa is built through defined roles, careful coordination, and a structured operational model designed for stability and responsible growth.

In its early development phase, the platform works with venue partners, sponsors, and institutional hosts to support delivery and growth.
