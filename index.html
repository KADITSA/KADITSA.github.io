ABOUT

Kaditsa is taking shape as a focused platform for presenting designers from across Asia within a clear and professional framework.

It was created around an observation rooted in experience — that across Asia, there are designers and studios producing strong, considered work that has yet to find its place on international platforms. Kaditsa is being built to create that context: structured, focused, and centered around the work itself.

At this stage, it is bringing together independent designers and small design studios, developing opportunities to present their work, connect with audiences, and build international visibility.

DESIGN WEEK BY KADITSA

Design Week by Kaditsa is a two-day design exhibition where selected designers present their work to audiences, buyers, and invited guests.

  • Up to 25 designers, each presenting selected pieces
  • VIP Preview Night and Public Exhibition Day
  • Private Buyer Preview — invitation only
  • Pre-order based sales model
  • Open to press, design institutions, and industry guests by invitation

The first edition will take place in Lisbon, Portugal, with future formats and editions to be developed over time.

STRUCTURE

Kaditsa is being developed through a structured approach designed for stability and responsible growth.

The platform builds through partnerships — working with venue partners, sponsors, and institutional hosts to develop each edition with operational and financial discipline.
